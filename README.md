# 🏥 Healthcare Patient Analytics Dashboard

> An end-to-end Healthcare Data Analytics project demonstrating data cleaning, SQL analysis, Python-based exploratory analysis, and interactive Power BI dashboard development.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Healthcare Patient Analytics Dashboard** analyzes hospital patient records from **2019–2024** to provide insights into patient demographics, medical conditions, hospital billing, admission trends, test results, and average Length of Stay (LOS).

This project demonstrates a complete data analytics workflow, from raw data preparation to business intelligence reporting.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Healthcare organizations generate large volumes of patient data every day. Without proper analysis, identifying treatment trends, billing patterns, patient demographics, and hospital performance becomes difficult.

This project transforms raw healthcare data into meaningful business insights that support informed decision-making.

# 💡 Key Business Insights

- Diabetes generated the highest overall billing amount.
- Patient admissions are almost equally distributed among Elective, Emergency, and Urgent categories.
- Arthritis, Diabetes, and Hypertension are among the most common medical conditions.
- Average Length of Stay ranges between **15–16 days** across major conditions.
- Adults between **40–60 years** account for the largest patient population.
- Test results are nearly evenly distributed across Normal, Abnormal, and Inconclusive outcomes.
